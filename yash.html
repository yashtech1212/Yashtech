Frequently Asked Questions

What image formats does PixelPerfect support?

PixelPerfect supports all major image formats including JPG, PNG, and WEBP. You can compress existing images or convert between these formats. WEBP typically offers the best compression with good quality retention.

Is there a limit to the file size I can compress?

There's no hard limit, but very large images (over 10MB) may take longer to process depending on your device's capabilities. For best performance, we recommend compressing images under 5MB each.

Where are my images stored during processing?

All processing happens directly in your browser. Your images never leave your device and are deleted from memory after processing or when you close the page. We don't store any of your images on our servers.

What quality setting should I use for web images?

For most web applications, we recommend using 70-80% quality. This provides excellent compression with minimal visible quality loss. For high-resolution displays or print materials, you may want to use 90% or higher quality.

Can I compress multiple images at once?

Yes! PixelPerfect supports batch processing. Simply select multiple files (or drag and drop them) and they'll all be compressed with your chosen settings. Each image is processed individually for optimal results.
